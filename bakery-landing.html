One oven, one family, no shortcuts.

Ember & Wheat started with a single clay oven built by hand in a backyard, and a starter carried over from a grandmother's kitchen. We still mix by hand, still shape every loaf ourselves, and still open the doors the moment the first batch comes out.

"We don't bake to sell out by noon. We bake to make the same loaf worth queuing for, every single morning."
